Preview

ADIA Computer Centre was founded in 2015 at Lagos, Nigeria. It is an IT training company that was developed with the aim of developing young students to become trained computer experts. ADIA Computer Centre estaablished their partnership with Aptech, a global IT training company and have been working together ever since.

About Us

ADIA Computer training is a computer training programme which co-exists with the Aptech foundation with the sole purpose of training young people in the fundamental and advanced knowledge of the computer and its software. This exercise enables the students participating to acquire some certain skills obtained through the knowledge of the computer to facilitate themselves and establish themselves quickly so as to become successful in the nearest future. As technology keeps on advancing, students need to be aware of how they can make good use of this opportunity by brandishing their computer troubleshooting skills and the likes, which enables them to obtain the intuition needed for solving technological problems in this day and age.
